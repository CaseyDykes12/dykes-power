## Post Format
Vary the format based on the topic. Do NOT use the same structure every day.
- **How-to guide**: numbered steps, practical and actionable
- **Comparison**: side-by-side, honest pros and cons
- **Advice piece**: conversational, like talking to a customer on the lot
- **Industry-specific**: written directly to that audience's world (golf supers, landscapers, etc.)

Length: 500–850 words. Some posts should be short and punchy. Some deeper.

Use subheadings naturally — not as a rigid template.
